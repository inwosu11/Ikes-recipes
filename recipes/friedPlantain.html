Cant have stew without Fried Plantain

OH! Plantain my first love!

DO'S

Please when making plantain make sure its ripe ripe. we dont like hard plantains over here. "PAUSE!" The edges of the plantain should be crisp. It should be sliced not diced when eaten with rice and stew this is not kele wele!

DONT'S

Dont dice it, this is not kele wele! Dont fry plantain thats not ripe, grill those ones. The plantain should not be super duper ripe to where its soggy.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Optional dry pepper

Let's Fry

  1. Slice the damn plantain, do not dice.
  2. Get a stainless steel pan
  3. fill the pan with oil to the thickness of two slices of plantains stacked on top of each other
  4. wait to the oil becomes half way hot and sizzles to the touch of any thing put in it
  5. Slide your plantains in one by one and do not over crowd
  6. flip once when the bottom side is golden brown and a bit dark on the edges and
  7. Enjoy your plantain!!!
